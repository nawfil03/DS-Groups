Job Description

Area Manager - GDK

Edinburgh

Posted 1 year ago

Description

Maintain high operational standards aligned with Domino's guidelines to drive efficient and profitable
business operations across all stores. Play an active role in developing the franchise's growth strategy
and implementing continuous improvement initiatives.
Key Accountabilities & Responsibilities

Customer Service and Sales Generation

· Pro-active and Re-active management of customer feedback/complaints
· Monitor and review service standards across stores taking corrective and preventative action where appropriate, and implementing measures to improve service standards
· Monitor and implement up-selling initiatives
· Ensure all Domino’s team members adhere to Domino’s image standards at all times
· Conduct random mystery shopper audits
· Analyse new sales and formulate area/regional plan to drive profitability

Scope

Responsible for 3 outlets with view to 5 sites total.

Additional Information

Be primarily based in the stores, working closely with the management team to enhance business
performance. Develop innovative strategies and address operational challenges. Collaborate with the
Franchisee/Directors to create business plans, track store progress, and implement new initiatives. This role
is adaptable and may require additional duties to meet the evolving needs of the business.
